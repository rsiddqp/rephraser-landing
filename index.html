How It Works

1

Select Text

Highlight any text in any app, or press Cmd+A to select all

2

Press Cmd+Shift+R

Window pops up above your text with rephrased version

3

Copy & Use

Press Cmd+C to copy and close, then paste wherever you need

Installation

  1. 1 Download Rephraser.zip
  2. 2 Extract and drag Rephraser.app to Applications
  3. 3 Right-click → Open (to bypass macOS warning)
  4. 4 Grant accessibility permissions when prompted
  5. 5 Done! Press Cmd+Shift+R on any selected text
